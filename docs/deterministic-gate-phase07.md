# Phase 07 Deterministic Gate

Use this checklist before claiming Agile artifacts are sprint-ready.

1. **Hybrid-Aware Inputs**
   - Sprint planning, DoR, and DoD read from the canonical project workspace under `projects/<ProjectName>/...`.
   - If the project is Hybrid, Agile artifacts reference the governing scope, compliance, or architectural constraints established upstream.

2. **Backlog Integrity**
   - Every sprint candidate links back to a user story, requirement, defect, or operational need.
   - Acceptance criteria and edge/error paths are present for each committed item.

3. **Deterministic Readiness and Done**
   - Definition of Ready includes explicit conditions for scope clarity, dependencies, testability, and compliance impact.
   - Definition of Done includes build, review, testing, documentation, and operational evidence checks.

4. **Traceability**
   - Sprint plan identifies which requirement IDs or business goals are being advanced.
   - Retrospective output captures action items with owners and due dates rather than narrative observations only.

5. **Exit Evidence**
   - A reviewer can determine why the sprint items were selected, what qualifies them as ready, and what proof is required for completion.
