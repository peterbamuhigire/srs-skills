# Deterministic Gate: Phase 07

## Scope

Phase 07 validates agile artefacts and their coupling to the baseline.

## Standard Anchor

- PMBOK Guide 7th Edition clause 2.6

## Enforced Checks

- `phase07.dor_references_baseline`
- `phase07.dod_references_compliance`
- `phase07.sprint_artifacts_have_ids`
- `phase07.retro_actions_assigned`
- `phase07.velocity_history_present`

## Intent

- Require DoR linkage to baseline IDs
- Require DoD linkage to compliance or quality constraints
- Require identifiable sprint backlog items
- Require owned and dated retrospective actions
- Require velocity evidence across more than one sprint

## Pass Condition

Agile execution stays connected to formal baseline scope, governance expectations, and continuous-improvement evidence.
