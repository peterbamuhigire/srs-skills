# Feature: Procurement & Contracts

## Description
Government acquisition lifecycle management — solicitation, vendor registration,
bid evaluation, contract award, performance tracking, and financial obligation
management — in compliance with FAR, DFARS, and relevant appropriations law.

## Standard Capabilities
- Procurement requisition and approval workflow
- Solicitation creation (RFQ, RFP, IFB) with public posting to SAM.gov or equivalent
- Vendor registration and System for Award Management (SAM) active registration verification
- Bid/proposal submission portal with deadline enforcement
- Evaluation scoring workflow with conflict-of-interest declaration
- Contract award notification and unsuccessful offeror notification
- Contract document management (base contract, modifications, task orders)
- Contract performance monitoring (deliverable tracking, acceptance workflow)
- Invoice receipt and approval workflow linked to contract line items
- USASpending.gov / national transparency reporting data feed
- Suspension and debarment check integration (SAM.gov exclusions)
- Protests and disputes tracking workflow

## Regulatory Hooks
- FAR (Federal Acquisition Regulation, 48 CFR Ch. 1): primary federal procurement regulation; all acquisitions must comply
- DFARS (Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement): additional requirements for DoD acquisitions
- Competition in Contracting Act (41 U.S.C. §3301): full and open competition required except where exception applies
- Anti-Deficiency Act (31 U.S.C. §1341): obligations must not exceed appropriated budget authority
- FAR 52.204-7: SAM registration required for all vendors receiving awards; system must verify before award
- FAR 4.1202: contract files must be retained per prescribed record retention periods

## Linked NFRs
- GOV-NFR-001 (FISMA Compliance — procurement system handles sensitive acquisition data)
- GOV-NFR-002 (Accessibility — vendor portal and public solicitation pages must be Section 508 compliant)
- GOV-NFR-003 (Data Sovereignty — contract and financial records must remain in national jurisdiction)
- GOV-NFR-005 (Audit Trail Retention — financial obligation records retained 7 years minimum)
