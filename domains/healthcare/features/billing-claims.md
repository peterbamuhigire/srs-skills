# Feature: Billing & Claims

## Description
End-to-end revenue cycle management — charge capture, coding, claim submission,
remittance processing, and patient billing.

## Standard Capabilities
- Charge capture from clinical encounters
- ICD-10-CM diagnosis coding
- CPT procedure coding
- CMS-1500 and UB-04 claim form generation
- Electronic claim submission (ANSI X12 837P/I)
- Electronic Remittance Advice processing (ANSI X12 835)
- Patient statement generation and payment portal
- Denial management and resubmission workflow
- Insurance verification and pre-authorization

## Regulatory Hooks
- CMS: must support ANSI X12 5010 transaction sets
- ICD-10 annual update cycle (October 1)
- HIPAA EDI transaction standards (45 CFR Part 162)

## Linked NFRs
- NFR-HC-001 (Audit Trail)
- NFR-HC-002 (Encryption at Rest)
- NFR-HC-007 (Data Retention — 6 years minimum)
