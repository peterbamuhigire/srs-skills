# Feature: Reporting & Analytics

## Description
Operational, clinical quality, and compliance reporting — census reports,
HEDIS measures, audit logs, and custom dashboards.

## Standard Capabilities
- Daily census and occupancy reports
- HEDIS quality measure calculation and reporting
- Meaningful Use / MIPS reporting exports
- Custom report builder with date range filtering
- Audit log query and export (compliance investigations)
- Financial summary and revenue cycle dashboards
- Patient population health analytics
- Scheduled report delivery (email/SFTP)

## Regulatory Hooks
- HIPAA: report exports containing PHI must be encrypted and access-logged
- CMS MIPS: annual quality reporting submission deadlines
- HEDIS: NCQA-defined measure specifications must be followed exactly

## Linked NFRs
- NFR-HC-001 (Audit Trail — all report exports logged)
- NFR-HC-008 (Breach Notification — breach impact reports)
