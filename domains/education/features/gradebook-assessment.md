# Feature: Gradebook & Assessment

## Description
Grade recording, assessment delivery, and academic performance reporting —
supporting formative and summative assessments, grade calculations, and
transcript generation with FERPA-compliant access controls.

## Standard Capabilities
- Grade entry and weighting configuration per course
- Automated grade calculation (weighted average, point-based, pass/fail)
- Online assessment delivery (multiple choice, short answer, file upload)
- Timed assessment controls with auto-submit on expiration
- Rubric creation and standards-based grading
- Grade override workflow with justification logging
- Interim and final grade reporting to students and parents
- Transcript generation (official, unofficial) with digital seal support
- Grade change audit trail with original and modified values
- Incomplete and withdrawal grade management

## Regulatory Hooks
- FERPA: grade records are education records; access restricted to authorized parties
- FERPA (34 CFR §99.31(a)(1)): teachers may only access grades for their own enrolled students
- Section 508 / WCAG 2.1 AA: assessment interfaces must be fully accessible including screen-reader-compatible question formats
- State reporting: grade data may require submission to state longitudinal data systems (SLDS)

## Linked NFRs
- EDU-NFR-001 (Student Record Confidentiality — grades are protected education records)
- EDU-NFR-002 (Accessibility Compliance — assessment delivery interfaces)
- EDU-NFR-004 (Data Retention — grade records retained 5 years post-graduation)
- EDU-NFR-005 (System Availability During Exam Periods)
