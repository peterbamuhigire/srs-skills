# Feature: Marketplace

## Description
Agricultural marketplace connecting farmers with buyers, agro-dealers, veterinary services, extension officers, and transport providers. Supports produce and livestock listings, market price tracking across Uganda markets, order management, cooperative bulk sales, and service provider directories with verified reviews.

## Standard Capabilities
- Produce and livestock listing creation with photos, quantity, grade, and price
- Buyer search and discovery with location and commodity filters
- Market price database tracking prices across Uganda markets (Kampala, Mbale, Gulu, Mbarara, etc.)
- Price alerts when market price exceeds or drops below farmer-set thresholds
- Agro-dealer directory with product catalogue and location
- Veterinary and extension officer directory with availability and ratings
- Transport provider matching based on route, load size, and vehicle type
- Order management (enquiry, negotiation, confirmation, delivery, payment)
- Verified buyer and seller reviews with transaction-linked ratings
- WhatsApp direct link for buyer-seller communication
- Cooperative bulk sales aggregation with member contribution tracking
- Seasonal supply and demand forecasting

## Regulatory Hooks
- Consumer protection regulations: accurate product descriptions and fair trading practices
