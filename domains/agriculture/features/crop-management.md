# Feature: Crop Management

## Description
Full crop lifecycle management from planning through harvest and yield analysis. Covers a 200+ crop library with local names, variety and cultivar tracking, season planning, activity recording, input tracking, crop health monitoring with photo-based AI pest and disease identification, harvest records, yield analysis, crop rotation planning, and season calendar integration.

## Standard Capabilities
- Crop library with 200+ crops including local/vernacular names and scientific names
- Variety and cultivar tracking with expected yield benchmarks
- Season planning with plot-crop-variety assignment
- Activity recording for 20+ activity types (planting, weeding, spraying, pruning, mulching, staking, thinning, fertilising, irrigating, scouting, etc.)
- Input tracking per activity (quantity, cost, application rate)
- Crop health monitoring with geotagged photo capture
- AI-powered pest and disease identification from photos
- Harvest recording (date, plot, quantity, grade, storage destination)
- Yield analysis with comparison against variety benchmarks and historical data
- Crop rotation planner with soil health recommendations
- Season calendar with region-specific planting windows
- Enterprise-level cost and revenue tracking per crop-season

## Regulatory Hooks
- MAAIF crop calendar: planting and activity recommendations aligned with Ministry of Agriculture seasonal guidance
- UCDA (Uganda Coffee Development Authority): compliance for coffee quality and traceability standards
- Codex Alimentarius: pesticide Maximum Residue Limits (MRLs) tracked per input application
