# Feature: Livestock Management

## Description
Individual animal tracking and herd/flock management across multiple species. Supports the complete livestock lifecycle including health events, reproductive tracking, production recording, feeding management, movement logging, sales, mortality, and pedigree tracking with breed-specific performance benchmarks.

## Standard Capabilities
- Species library (cattle, goats, sheep, pigs, chickens, ducks, turkeys, rabbits, bees, fish)
- Individual animal registration with unique identifier, photo, and tag number
- Herd and flock grouping with batch operations
- Breed tracking with production benchmarks (milk yield, weight gain, egg rate)
- Health event recording (vaccination, treatment, deworming, veterinary visits)
- Reproductive event tracking (heat detection, insemination, pregnancy check, birth, weaning)
- Production records (milk yield per session, egg collection, honey harvest, fish harvest)
- Feed management (ration formulation, feed consumption, cost per animal/group)
- Movement log (paddock rotation, market transport, sale transfer)
- Sales recording with buyer details and price per unit
- Mortality recording with cause of death classification
- Pedigree tracking with sire/dam lineage and inbreeding coefficient

## Regulatory Hooks
- NLIS/UIA animal identification: compliance with national livestock identification and traceability systems
- Veterinary drug regulations: withdrawal period tracking for treated animals before slaughter or milk sale
- Animal Disease Act: notifiable disease reporting obligations
