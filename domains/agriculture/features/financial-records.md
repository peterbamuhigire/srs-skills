# Feature: Financial Records

## Description
Dual-mode farm financial management supporting both simple Money In/Out tracking for smallholders and advanced double-entry accounting for commercial farms. Covers income and expense recording with enterprise linkage, budgeting, profitability analysis, cash flow, market prices, loan tracking, mobile money integration, and credibility reporting for bank loan applications.

## Standard Capabilities
- Dual-mode accounting (Simple: Money In/Out; Advanced: double-entry with chart of accounts)
- Income and expense recording with enterprise linkage (crop, livestock, plot)
- Budget planning and tracking per enterprise and season
- Profitability analysis per enterprise (revenue minus allocated costs)
- Cash flow statement generation (monthly, quarterly, seasonal)
- Market price database with regional price tracking
- Loan tracking for SACCO, MFI, and bank loans (disbursement, repayment schedule, balance)
- Mobile money payment tracking (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money) with transaction reference
- Receipt and invoice photo upload with OCR extraction
- Bank loan credibility report generation (income summary, asset register, repayment history)
- Invoice and receipt generation for produce sales
- Payroll calculation for farm workers (daily, weekly, monthly)

## Regulatory Hooks
- Employment Act 2006: payroll records must comply with statutory requirements for wages, overtime, and leave
- NSSF Act: mandatory employer and employee contributions for permanent staff
- PAYE (Pay As You Earn): income tax deduction and remittance for qualifying employees

## Linked NFRs

- AG-003 (Financial Data Accuracy and Reconciliation)
