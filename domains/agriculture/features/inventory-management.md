# Feature: Inventory Management

## Description
Comprehensive farm input and output inventory tracking covering seeds, fertilisers, pesticides, animal feed, veterinary medicine, equipment, and harvested produce. Supports batch and expiry tracking, automatic stock deduction on activity recording, low stock and expiry alerts, equipment maintenance scheduling, and post-harvest loss monitoring.

## Standard Capabilities
- Input inventory management (seeds, fertilisers, pesticides, feed, veterinary medicine)
- Stock receipt recording with batch number, expiry date, supplier, and cost
- Automatic stock deduction when inputs are recorded against crop or livestock activities
- Low stock threshold alerts (push notification, SMS)
- Expiry date alerts for perishable inputs and medicines
- Equipment and tools inventory with asset register
- Equipment maintenance scheduling with service history
- Fuel consumption log for machinery and vehicles
- Harvest produce inventory tracked per storage location (store, silo, cold room)
- Post-harvest loss tracking by cause (pest damage, moisture, spoilage, theft)
- Stock valuation using FIFO or weighted average methods
- Stock movement report (receipts, issues, adjustments, balances)

## Regulatory Hooks
- NAADS (National Agricultural Advisory Services): voucher tracking for government-subsidised input distribution programmes
