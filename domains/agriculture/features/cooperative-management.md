# Feature: Cooperative Management

## Description
Multi-tenant cooperative franchise management enabling cooperatives to onboard member farmers, manage collection centres, track bulk input distribution, calculate member payments by quantity and grade, and disburse payments via mobile money. Includes field agent tools for member registration and GPS mapping, aggregate supply reporting, and farmer performance ranking.

## Standard Capabilities
- Cooperative registration as franchise tenant with administrative hierarchy
- Member farmer registration with profile, NIN, mobile money number, and farm details
- Member farm GPS mapping via field agent mobile app
- Bulk input distribution tracking (seed, fertiliser, pesticide allocation per member)
- Collection centre management (weighing, grading, lot assignment)
- Member payment calculation (quantity multiplied by grade-specific price)
- Mobile money bulk disbursement with transaction confirmation and reconciliation
- Aggregate supply reports (total collected by commodity, grade, period, region)
- Compliance monitoring (member adherence to cooperative standards and certification requirements)
- Field agent simplified mobile app for registration, GPS capture, and collection
- Farmer performance ranking by yield, quality, and compliance metrics
- Season advance and input credit tracking with deduction at payment
- Cooperative-level financial summary and member statement generation

## Regulatory Hooks
- Uganda Cooperative Societies Act: governance, reporting, and audit requirements for registered cooperatives
- NAADS/OWC (Operation Wealth Creation): alignment with government agricultural programme distribution and reporting requirements

## Linked NFRs
- AG-001 (Data Integrity and Audit Trail)
- AG-003 (Financial Data Accuracy and Reconciliation)
