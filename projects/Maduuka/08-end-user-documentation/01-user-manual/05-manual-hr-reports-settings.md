# HR, Reports, and Settings

## Adding a Staff Member

1. Go to **More** → **HR** → **Staff**.
2. Tap the **+** button.
3. Fill in the staff profile:
   - **Full Name**
   - **Phone Number** — used to send payslips via WhatsApp
   - **National ID Number (NIN)**
   - **Department** and **Job Title**
   - **Branch** — the branch where this staff member works
   - **Employment Type** — Full-Time, Part-Time, or Contract
   - **Hire Date**
4. Under **Salary Structure**, enter:
   - **Basic Salary** — the monthly base pay
   - Any allowances (e.g., transport, housing) — tap **Add Allowance** for each
   - Any deductions beyond tax (e.g., staff loans) — tap **Add Deduction**
5. Tap **Save Staff Member**.

Maduuka automatically calculates PAYE (income tax), NSSF (employee 5%, employer 10%), and LST (Local Service Tax) based on the salary entered. You do not calculate these manually.

## Approving a Leave Request

Staff can apply for leave through the Maduuka app on their phone. As a manager, you receive an alert.

1. On the **Home** dashboard, tap the **Pending Approvals** card. Or go to **More** → **HR** → **Leave Requests**.
2. Tap the leave request to open it. You will see: staff name, leave type, dates requested, and remaining leave days.
3. Review the request.
4. Tap **Approve** or **Reject**.
5. If rejecting, enter a short reason.

The staff member receives a notification of your decision on their phone.

## Running the Monthly Payroll

Run payroll once at the end of each month, or on the date agreed in your staff contracts.

1. Go to **More** → **HR** → **Payroll**.
2. Tap **New Payroll Run**.
3. Select the **Month** and **Year**.
4. Maduuka calculates pay for all active staff automatically, including PAYE, NSSF, and any loan deductions.
5. Review each staff member's pay summary. If you need to add a one-off bonus or extra deduction for this month only, tap the staff member's name and tap **Add Adjustment**.
6. When you are satisfied, tap **Submit for Approval**.
7. The business owner or authorised approver reviews and taps **Approve Payroll**.
8. After approval, tap **Generate Payslips** to create PDF payslips for all staff.

*Note: Once a payroll run is approved, you cannot edit it. If you made a mistake, see the FAQ section on correcting an approved payroll.*

## Sending Payslips via WhatsApp

After approving a payroll run:

1. Go to **More** → **HR** → **Payroll**.
2. Tap the approved payroll run.
3. Tap **Send Payslips**.
4. Select **WhatsApp** as the delivery method.
5. Review the list of staff — each staff member's phone number is shown.
6. Tap **Send All** to send all payslips at once, or tap individual names to send one at a time.

Each staff member receives their payslip as a PDF attached to a WhatsApp message from your business number.

## Reading the Daily Sales Report

The daily sales report shows you everything that happened on a specific day.

1. Go to **More** → **Reports** → **Daily Sales Report**.
2. Select the **Date**. The current day is selected by default.
3. The report shows:
   - **Total Revenue** — total value of all sales for the day
   - **Cash Collected** — cash received
   - **MoMo Collected** — mobile money received
   - **Credit Sales** — sales made on credit (not yet collected)
   - **Number of Transactions** — how many sales were made
   - **Voids** — any sales that were cancelled
   - **Top 5 Products** — the 5 products that sold the most
4. Tap any line to see the detailed transactions behind it.

## Exporting a Report to CSV or PDF

Any report in Maduuka can be exported.

1. Open the report you want to export (for example, **Daily Sales Report** or **Debtors Ageing**).
2. Set your date range and any filters.
3. Tap **Export** at the top right of the report.
4. Choose **Export to PDF** or **Export to CSV**.
   - PDF — a formatted file you can print or share as a document
   - CSV — a file that opens in Excel or Google Sheets for further analysis
5. The file downloads to your phone or computer. On Android, find it in your **Downloads** folder.

## Changing Your Business Name and Logo

1. Go to **More** → **Settings** → **Business Profile**.
2. Tap **Business Name** and type the new name.
3. To change the logo, tap the current logo image and select **Change Logo**. Choose a photo from your gallery.
4. Tap **Save Changes**.

The new name and logo will appear on all future receipts and PDF invoices.

## Setting Up 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) for Your Account

2-factor authentication (2FA) means you need both your password and a one-time code to log in. It protects your account if someone else learns your password.

1. Go to **More** → **Settings** → **My Account** → **Security**.
2. Tap **Enable 2-Factor Authentication**.
3. Download an authenticator app on your phone if you do not have one — for example, **Google Authenticator** (free on Google Play Store).
4. Open Google Authenticator. Tap **+** → **Scan QR code**.
5. Point your camera at the QR code shown in Maduuka.
6. Google Authenticator now shows a 6-digit code that changes every 30 seconds.
7. Enter the 6-digit code in the Maduuka confirmation box.
8. Tap **Confirm**. 2FA is now active.

From the next login onwards, after entering your password you will be asked for the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.

*Keep your phone safe. If you lose your phone, contact your business owner to reset your 2FA.*
