# Getting Started with Maduuka

## What is Maduuka?

Maduuka is a business management app for African shops, pharmacies, restaurants, and any business that sells goods or services. You use it on your Android phone or in a web browser. It records every sale, tracks your stock, manages your customers, and shows you how much money your business made today — all in one place.

Maduuka works even when you have no internet. Everything you do — sales, stock entries, payments — is saved on your phone and automatically sent to the server the moment your internet returns. Your data is never lost.

## Who Uses Maduuka?

Maduuka has six roles. Each role sees only the screens that are relevant to their job.

- **Business Owner** — full access to all features, reports, and settings
- **Branch Manager** — manages one branch: sales, stock, staff approvals, and branch reports
- **Cashier** — operates the point of sale, processes sales and payments, prints receipts
- **Stock Keeper** — manages products, receives deliveries, runs stock counts, transfers stock
- **Accountant** — views financial reports, manages expenses, reconciles accounts, exports data
- **HR Manager** — manages staff profiles, leave, attendance, and payroll

## How to Log In

### On Android

1. Open the **Maduuka** app on your phone.
2. Enter your **Email** or **Phone Number**.
3. Enter your **Password**.
4. Tap **Log In**.
5. If your business owner has enabled 2-step verification, enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.

### On the Web

1. Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari).
2. Go to your business Maduuka URL — for example, `yourshop.maduuka.com`.
3. Enter your **Email** or **Phone Number**.
4. Enter your **Password**.
5. Click **Log In**.

If you forget your password, tap **Forgot Password?** and enter your email. A reset link will be sent to you.

## How to Navigate

### On Android (Bottom Tabs)

At the bottom of the screen you will see 5 tabs:

- **Home** — your dashboard with today's sales summary and alerts
- **POS** — the point of sale screen for making sales
- **Stock** — inventory, products, and stock counts
- **Reports** — sales, expenses, and financial reports
- **More** — customers, suppliers, HR, settings, and everything else

Tap any tab to switch to that section. The tab you are on is shown in blue.

### On the Web (Left Sidebar)

On the left side of the screen is the main menu. Click any item to open it. Sub-items expand when you click the arrow next to a menu item. The top bar shows your business name, branch, and your profile icon.

## Understanding Your Role: What You Can and Cannot Do

| Role | POS | Stock | Customers | Reports | HR | Settings |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business Owner | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full | Full |
| Branch Manager | Full | Full | Full | Branch only | Branch only | Limited |
| Cashier | Full | View only | View only | None | None | None |
| Stock Keeper | None | Full | None | Stock only | None | None |
| Accountant | View only | View only | Full | Full | None | Limited |
| HR Manager | None | None | None | HR only | Full | Limited |

If you tap a button and see "You do not have permission to do this," it means that action requires a higher role. Ask your manager or business owner.

## How Offline Mode Works

If you have no internet, Maduuka still works. Your sales are saved on your phone and sent to the server when internet returns.

Here is what happens step by step:

1. You lose internet — Maduuka shows a small orange bar at the top of the screen: **"Offline — working locally."**
2. You continue making sales as normal. Everything is saved on your phone.
3. When internet returns, the orange bar turns green: **"Syncing..."** and then disappears.
4. All offline sales, stock movements, and payments are uploaded automatically.

You do not need to do anything. Maduuka syncs by itself.

*Note: Some features require internet — for example, sending a receipt via SMS or processing a mobile money payment. These will show a message asking you to wait for connectivity.*
