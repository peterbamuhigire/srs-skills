# POS Module: Complete Guide

The Point of Sale (POS) module is where you record every sale your business makes. This guide takes you through every step from opening your shift to closing it.

## Opening a POS Session

A *POS session* is your cashier shift. You must open a session before you can make a sale. When you open a session, you record the cash already in the till — this is called the *opening float*.

1. Tap the **POS** tab at the bottom of the screen.
2. Tap **Open Session**.
3. Count the cash in your till.
4. Enter the amount in the **Opening Float** field — for example, `50,000`.
5. Tap **Confirm and Open Session**.

The POS screen opens and you are ready to sell.

## Making a Sale

### Finding a Product

You can find a product in 3 ways:

**By name:** Type the product name in the **Search** bar at the top. Results appear as you type.

**By barcode:** Tap the **barcode icon** next to the search bar. Point your phone camera at the barcode on the product. Maduuka reads it automatically and adds the product to the cart. *Tip: Make sure there is enough light for the camera to read the barcode clearly.*

**By category:** Below the search bar, tap a category tab (e.g., **Beverages**, **Grains**, **Household**). Products in that category appear in the grid. Tap the product to add it.

### Adding Items to the Cart and Changing Quantities

1. Tap a product from the search results or grid. It appears in the cart on the right (or bottom on some screens).
2. To add the same product again, tap it again — the quantity increases by 1 each time.
3. To set an exact quantity, tap the product in the cart, then tap the **quantity number**. Type the quantity and tap **Done**.
4. To remove a product from the cart, tap the product in the cart and tap **Remove**.

## Applying a Discount

### Discount on One Item

1. In the cart, tap the item you want to discount.
2. Tap **Discount**.
3. Enter the discount — type a number followed by `%` for a percentage (e.g., `10%`) or type a flat amount (e.g., `2,000`).
4. Tap **Apply**. The discounted price shows on the item line.

### Discount on the Whole Order

1. Tap **Order Discount** at the bottom of the cart (below the item list).
2. Enter the discount amount or percentage.
3. Tap **Apply**. The discount is spread across the whole order and the new total is shown.

## Holding a Sale and Resuming It Later

If a customer needs to go and get more items or money, you can hold the sale and serve another customer in the meantime.

1. Tap **Hold** (shown as a pause icon or labelled **Hold Sale**).
2. The cart is saved and the POS clears for a new sale.
3. To resume, tap **Held Sales** at the top of the POS screen.
4. Find the held sale in the list and tap **Resume**.

## Accepting Payment

### Cash Payment

1. After adding all items to the cart, tap **Charge** or **Accept Payment**.
2. Select **Cash**.
3. In the **Amount Tendered** field, enter the money the customer gives you — for example, `50,000`.
4. Maduuka calculates the change automatically and shows it in large text: **"Change: UGX 12,500."**
5. Give the customer their change.
6. Tap **Confirm Sale**.

### MTN MoMo Payment

1. Tap **Charge** or **Accept Payment**.
2. Select **MTN MoMo**.
3. Enter the customer's **MTN phone number** (e.g., `0771234567`).
4. Tap **Send Payment Request**.
5. The customer receives a prompt on their phone. They enter their MoMo PIN to confirm.
6. Maduuka waits for confirmation. When the payment is received, the screen shows **"Payment Successful."**
7. Tap **Confirm Sale**.

*Note: The customer must have enough MoMo balance and must confirm within 90 seconds. If they do not confirm in time, the request times out — see the FAQ for what to do.*

### Airtel Money Payment

The steps are the same as MTN MoMo:

1. Tap **Charge** or **Accept Payment**.
2. Select **Airtel Money**.
3. Enter the customer's **Airtel phone number**.
4. Tap **Send Payment Request**.
5. The customer receives a prompt. They confirm with their Airtel Money PIN.
6. When confirmed, the screen shows **"Payment Successful."**
7. Tap **Confirm Sale**.

### Credit Sale

A credit sale means the customer takes goods now and pays later. The customer must have a credit account in Maduuka.

1. Tap **Charge** or **Accept Payment**.
2. Select **Credit**.
3. Select the customer from the list or search by name or phone.
4. The customer's available credit limit is shown. If the sale amount is within the limit, tap **Confirm Sale**.

**If the credit limit is reached:** A message appears: **"Credit limit exceeded."** You have two options:
- Ask the customer to pay in cash or MoMo for the balance.
- Ask your manager to approve an override. The manager taps **Approve Override** and enters their PIN.

### Split Payment (Part Cash, Part MoMo)

A split payment lets one customer pay with two or more methods.

1. Tap **Charge** or **Accept Payment**.
2. Tap **Split Payment**.
3. Select the first payment method (e.g., **Cash**) and enter the amount the customer is paying in cash.
4. Tap **Add Another Payment**.
5. Select the second method (e.g., **MTN MoMo**) and enter the remaining amount.
6. Maduuka confirms when the total collected equals the sale total.
7. Tap **Confirm Sale**.

## Printing and Sending a Receipt

After confirming a sale, the receipt options appear:

- **Print on thermal printer** — tap **Print Receipt**. The receipt prints on your Bluetooth or USB 80mm thermal printer.
- **Send via WhatsApp** — tap **Send WhatsApp**. Enter the customer's WhatsApp number. The receipt is sent as a message.
- **Send via SMS** — tap **Send SMS**. Enter the customer's phone number. A text receipt is sent.
- **No receipt** — tap **Done** or **Skip**.

## Closing a POS Session (End of Shift)

At the end of your shift, you reconcile the till — you count the actual cash and compare it to what Maduuka recorded.

1. Tap **Close Session** (found in the POS menu or the **More** tab).
2. Maduuka shows a summary: total sales, cash expected, MoMo collected, credit sales.
3. Count the physical cash in the till.
4. Enter the **Actual Cash** amount in the field provided.
5. Maduuka calculates the **difference**. If the actual cash matches, the difference shows `0`.
6. If there is a difference, enter a **note** explaining the discrepancy (e.g., "Gave UGX 5,000 change from my own pocket for small change").
7. Tap **Close Session**.

**If cash does not match:** Do not panic. Record the actual amount you counted. The system logs the difference. Your manager can review the session report to investigate. Never adjust the figure to make it match — this hides the discrepancy from the report.

## Voiding a Sale

A void cancels a sale that was already recorded. You can void a sale from the current session.

1. In the POS screen, tap **Sales History** or go to **More** → **Sales History**.
2. Find the sale you want to void.
3. Tap the sale to open it.
4. Tap **Void Sale**.
5. Select a **reason** from the list (e.g., "Customer changed mind," "Wrong items added," "Payment failed").
6. Tap **Confirm Void**.

*Why a reason is required:* Every void is recorded in the audit trail with a timestamp, the cashier's name, and the reason. This protects honest cashiers and helps the owner investigate unusual patterns.

*Note: You can only void sales from the current open session. Voids from a previous session require a manager's approval.*
