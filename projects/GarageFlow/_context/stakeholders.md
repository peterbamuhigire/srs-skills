## Tenant-side primary stakeholders

- **Garage owner / CEO** — subscribes, configures, monitors business performance. Primary decision-maker for upgrades and add-ons.
- **Branch manager** — runs a branch day-to-day, approves adjustments, owns quality control.
- **Service advisor** — customer-facing, composes estimates, captures approvals.
- **Workshop controller** — assigns jobs to bays and technicians on the bay board.
- **Technician** — executes work, captures inspection findings, clocks on/off jobs.
- **Storekeeper** — receives parts, issues to jobs, runs cycle counts.
- **Accountant** — reviews GL postings, reconciles, runs periodic close.
- **Payroll officer** — runs payroll, uses labour-linked productivity data.

## Tenant-side external users

- **Individual vehicle owner** — uses Customer App.
- **Fleet manager** — manages many vehicles for one corporate customer.
- **Corporate account administrator** — approves invoices on behalf of a corporate customer.
