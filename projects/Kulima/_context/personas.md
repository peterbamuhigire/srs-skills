# User Personas

## Nakato Grace (Smallholder Farmer)
- **Age / Background:** 38, widow, P.7 education, manages 3 acres in Mbarara growing matooke, beans, and keeping 5 local Ankole cattle and 30 kienyeji chickens
- **Goals:** Keep records of what she plants, harvests, and sells so she can apply for a SACCO loan. Know when to vaccinate her cattle. Track egg production to understand profitability
- **Pain Points:** Currently uses a school exercise book that got wet and lost 6 months of records. Cannot calculate profit per enterprise. Missed a vaccination date and lost a calf to East Coast Fever
- **Tech Comfort:** Low — uses a Tecno Spark (Android, 32GB storage, 2GB RAM). Comfortable with MTN MoMo and WhatsApp. Prefers Luganda interface
- **Typical Workflow:** Wakes at 6am, milks cows, collects eggs, feeds chickens, works in matooke garden. Records are done at night by paraffin lamp — needs the app to work offline and be usable in dim light with large touch targets

## Mugisha Robert (Commercial Farmer)
- **Age / Background:** 52, university-educated, owns a 200-acre coffee estate in Kasese and a 50-acre cattle ranch in Mbarara. Employs 15 permanent staff and 30+ seasonal workers
- **Goals:** Professional financial reporting for his bank (Centenary) to expand his loan facility. Track input costs per acre to optimise coffee production. Manage workers and payroll across two farms from Kampala
- **Pain Points:** Uses Excel spreadsheets managed by an accountant who visits once a month. Cannot see real-time farm performance from Kampala. Workers report hours manually with no verification. Wants CCTV on both farms to monitor remotely
- **Tech Comfort:** High — uses iPhone 15, laptop, comfortable with English. Needs multi-device sync
- **Typical Workflow:** Checks farm dashboards from Kampala office each morning. Reviews worker task completion, financial summary, and any IoT alerts. Visits farms fortnightly. Needs director-level consolidated view across both farms

## Apio Sarah (Cooperative Programme Manager)
- **Age / Background:** 45, manages Acholi Organic Coffee Cooperative in Gulu with 320 member farmers across 4 sub-counties. Funded by an NGO to digitise operations
- **Goals:** Register all 320 member farmers with GPS farm boundaries. Track input distribution (seedlings, organic fertiliser). Manage collection centres during harvest season. Pay members via mobile money based on quantity and quality delivered. Generate EUDR compliance reports for their European buyer
- **Pain Points:** Uses paper registers at each collection point. Payment calculations take 3 days by hand. Cannot verify which members actually received subsidised inputs. Their European buyer is demanding EUDR traceability and they have no system
- **Tech Comfort:** Medium — uses Samsung A14 (Android), comfortable with English and Acholi. Her field agents have mixed smartphone literacy
- **Typical Workflow:** Manages from Gulu town office. Sends field agents to visit member farms weekly. During harvest (twice yearly), runs 4 collection centres simultaneously. Needs bulk payment at season end

## Katumba James (Farm Director / Investor)
- **Age / Background:** 60, retired banker, has invested in 3 farms managed by different farm managers: dairy farm in Entebbe, poultry farm in Mukono, vanilla estate in Bundibugyo
- **Goals:** See consolidated financial performance across all 3 investments. Approve major purchases (above UGX 5M). Get alerts when anything goes wrong (disease outbreak, security breach, budget overrun)
- **Pain Points:** Each farm manager sends him a WhatsApp summary monthly — no standard format, no way to verify numbers. Suspects one manager is inflating expenses. Cannot compare performance across farms
- **Tech Comfort:** Medium — uses iPhone, prefers English, wants simple dashboards not detailed operational screens
- **Typical Workflow:** Checks director dashboard once daily (5 minutes). Reviews and approves pending purchase requests. Reads monthly consolidated P&L. Visits each farm quarterly

## Ocan David (Farm Worker)
- **Age / Background:** 24, S.4 education, casual worker on Mugisha's coffee estate in Kasese. Paid daily wage of UGX 15,000 via MTN MoMo
- **Goals:** See what tasks he's assigned for the day. Mark tasks complete so his hours are recorded accurately. Receive his daily pay on time
- **Pain Points:** Sometimes the supervisor forgets to record his hours and he loses a day's pay. Tasks are given verbally and he forgets details
- **Tech Comfort:** Low-Medium — uses Itel A18 (Android Go, 16GB storage). Comfortable with MoMo. Prefers Luganda or English
- **Typical Workflow:** Arrives at farm at 7am. Checks phone for task list. Does assigned work (spraying, weeding, harvesting). Marks each task complete on phone. Checks that hours are logged correctly at end of day

## Van der Berg Hans (European Coffee Buyer)
- **Age / Background:** 48, specialty coffee buyer for a German roasting company. Sources Arabica from Kasese and Robusta from Luwero
- **Goals:** Verify that coffee he purchases meets EUDR deforestation-free requirements. Access farm origin story and input history for each batch. Verify organic/RainForest Alliance certifications
- **Pain Points:** Currently relies on paper certificates and email chains from cooperative managers. Auditing is expensive and slow. Needs digital traceability before his company's EUDR compliance deadline
- **Tech Comfort:** High — uses laptop and smartphone, English and German
- **Typical Workflow:** Receives QR code on shipment documentation. Scans it to view Kulima buyer portal: farm GPS polygon, deforestation check, input history, certifications, chain of custody. Downloads EUDR DDS report for his compliance team
