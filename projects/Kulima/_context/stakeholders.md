# Stakeholders

## Individual Farmer (Smallholder)
- **Influence:** Medium
- **Interest:** High
- **Primary Needs:** Simple digital records replacing paper notebooks, offline-capable app on low-spec Android, local language interface, affordable pricing (free or UGX 40,000/month), weather forecasts, crop and livestock activity tracking
- **Key Concerns:** Data loss, complexity, internet dependency, cost, phone storage space
- **Communication Preference:** In-app notifications, SMS alerts, WhatsApp

## Farm Business / Company
- **Influence:** High
- **Interest:** High
- **Primary Needs:** Multi-user access with role-based permissions, formal accounting (double-entry), staff management with payroll, professional reports for investors and banks, multi-farm management
- **Key Concerns:** Data security, reporting accuracy, staff accountability, regulatory compliance
- **Communication Preference:** Email, in-app dashboard, scheduled PDF reports

## Cooperative / NGO / Programme
- **Influence:** High
- **Interest:** High
- **Primary Needs:** Member farmer registration and management, bulk input distribution tracking, collection centre operations, quality grading, collective sales with automated payment distribution to members via mobile money, compliance monitoring, field agent mobile app
- **Key Concerns:** Member data accuracy, payment transparency, programme compliance reporting, scale (hundreds to thousands of members)
- **Communication Preference:** WhatsApp, email, programme dashboard

## Farm Director / Investor
- **Influence:** High
- **Interest:** Medium
- **Primary Needs:** Consolidated multi-farm dashboard, financial summary across portfolio, approval workflows for major decisions, IoT health alerts aggregated, harvest forecasts
- **Key Concerns:** Portfolio performance visibility, fraud prevention, ROI tracking
- **Communication Preference:** Director mobile app, email summaries, critical alerts via SMS

## Buyer / Off-taker
- **Influence:** Medium
- **Interest:** Medium
- **Primary Needs:** Verifiable traceability data via QR code scan, EUDR compliance proof, farmer and farm origin story, input history for the batch, certification verification
- **Key Concerns:** Traceability authenticity, regulatory compliance, supply reliability
- **Communication Preference:** Buyer portal (web), QR code scan results

## Farm Worker
- **Influence:** Low
- **Interest:** Medium
- **Primary Needs:** View assigned tasks, mark tasks complete, see daily work hours logged, receive wage payment via mobile money
- **Key Concerns:** Wage accuracy, task clarity, phone battery and data usage
- **Communication Preference:** SMS, push notifications, simplified worker app interface

## Jaguza (IoT Partner)
- **Influence:** Medium
- **Interest:** High
- **Primary Needs:** API integration for their ear tag devices, expanded market for their hardware through Kulima's user base, data displayed in Kulima without requiring Jaguza's own app
- **Key Concerns:** API reliability, data accuracy in Kulima display, brand visibility, commercial terms
- **Communication Preference:** Technical API documentation, partnership meetings

## Banks / SACCOs / MFIs
- **Influence:** Medium
- **Interest:** Medium
- **Primary Needs:** Standardised farm financial data for loan risk assessment, farm asset records, production history, repayment capacity indicators
- **Key Concerns:** Data reliability, fraud prevention, standardised report format matching their loan evaluation criteria
- **Communication Preference:** Formal reports (PDF/Excel), API integration (Phase 4)

## MAAIF / Government Extension Services
- **Influence:** Medium
- **Interest:** Low
- **Primary Needs:** Aggregate agricultural data for policy planning, extension service delivery channel, NAADS/OWC programme tracking
- **Key Concerns:** Data privacy of individual farmers, programme compliance, reporting accuracy
- **Communication Preference:** Formal data sharing agreements, aggregate reports

## Agro-Dealers / Input Suppliers
- **Influence:** Low
- **Interest:** Medium
- **Primary Needs:** Visibility in the agro-dealer directory, sales channel through marketplace, input voucher redemption tracking
- **Key Concerns:** Directory listing accuracy, payment processing, competition
- **Communication Preference:** Marketplace listing management, WhatsApp

## Veterinary Officers / Extension Officers
- **Influence:** Low
- **Interest:** Medium
- **Primary Needs:** Visibility in the vet/extension directory, referral channel from AI advisor escalation, farm visit scheduling
- **Key Concerns:** Professional credibility, service area accuracy
- **Communication Preference:** Directory listing, referral notifications
