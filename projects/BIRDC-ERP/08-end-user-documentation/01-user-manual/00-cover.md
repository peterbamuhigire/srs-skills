## About This Manual

This manual covers the daily operations that each staff role performs in the BIRDC ERP system. Instructions are written in plain language. Every procedure is numbered step by step so you can follow along without prior training.

**How to use this manual:**

- Find your role in the table of contents.
- Follow each numbered step in order.
- If the screen looks different from what the manual describes, contact your system administrator.

**Important terms used in this manual:**

- *Session* — the period from when you open the cash register to when you close it at the end of your shift.
- *Sync* — the process of uploading data from your phone to the main server.
- *EFRIS* — the Uganda Revenue Authority system that records every sale you make. The ERP submits to EFRIS automatically.
- *Agent cash balance* — the total cash an agent owes BIRDC: all sales collected minus all remittances verified.
- *Vote* — a parliamentary budget category (used in PIBID government accounting).

For a full list of terms, see the Glossary at the end of this manual.
