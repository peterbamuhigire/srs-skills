# Section 3: Grace — Finance Director

Grace uses the main ERP web application. She reviews, approves, and generates financial reports for both PIBID parliamentary accounts and BIRDC commercial accounts.

---

## 3.1 Approving Journal Entries

Journal entries created by accountants require Finance Director approval before they are posted to the General Ledger.

1. Log in to the ERP and click **Finance** in the main menu.
2. Click **Journal Entries**.
3. Click the **Pending Approval** tab. All journal entries waiting for your review are listed.
4. Click on a journal entry to open it.
5. Review the debit and credit lines, the account codes, and the description. Confirm the entry is correct.
6. If the entry is correct, click **Approve & Post**. The entry is posted to the General Ledger and cannot be changed.
7. If the entry is incorrect, click **Reject** and type the reason in the **Rejection Notes** box. The entry is returned to the creator for correction.

*You cannot create and approve the same journal entry. This is a system control — a different user must create the entry for you to approve it.*

## 3.2 Running a Payroll

*Ensure all employee attendance, leave deductions, and advances have been recorded before running payroll.*

1. Click **Payroll** in the main menu.
2. Click **New Payroll Run**.
3. Select the **Payroll Period** (month and year).
4. Select the group of employees: **PIBID Staff**, **BIRDC Staff**, or **Casual Workers**.
5. Click **Calculate**. The system computes gross pay, PAYE, NSSF, LST, and all other deductions for every employee.
6. Review the summary table. Check totals for gross pay, PAYE, NSSF, and net pay.
7. Click on any employee's name to see their individual payslip breakdown.
8. If everything is correct, click **Approve & Lock Payroll Run**.
9. A confirmation prompt asks you to confirm. Click **Yes, Lock**.
10. The payroll run is locked. No changes can be made to it after this point.
11. The system generates the GL posting (payroll expense entry) automatically.
12. To release payslips, click **Send Payslips** — choose **Email** or **WhatsApp**.

*Once approved and locked, a payroll run cannot be edited. If you find an error after locking, process a correction in the next payroll period.*

## 3.3 Viewing the Trial Balance

The Trial Balance is available at any time. No period closing is required.

**Parliamentary Mode (PIBID):**

1. Click **Finance** then **Reports**.
2. Click **Trial Balance**.
3. In the **Accounting Mode** field, select **PIBID Parliamentary**.
4. Select the date range.
5. Click **Generate**.
6. The report shows all PIBID parliamentary accounts, opening balances, period movements, and closing balances.

**Commercial Mode (BIRDC):**

1. Follow steps 1–2 above.
2. In the **Accounting Mode** field, select **BIRDC Commercial**.
3. Select the date range.
4. Click **Generate**.

**Consolidated View (both modes):**

1. In the **Accounting Mode** field, select **Consolidated**.
2. The report shows parliamentary and commercial accounts side by side, clearly labelled.

To export the Trial Balance to Excel, click **Export to Excel** at the top right of the report.

## 3.4 Generating a Profit and Loss Report

1. Click **Finance** then **Reports**.
2. Click **Profit & Loss**.
3. Select the **Accounting Mode**: BIRDC Commercial or Consolidated.
4. Select the date range (start date and end date).
5. Click **Generate**.
6. The P&L report appears showing revenue, cost of goods sold, gross profit, operating expenses, and net profit.
7. To compare against a prior period, click **Add Comparison Period** and select the prior period.
8. Click **Export to Excel** or **Export to PDF** to save a copy.

---

## 3.5 Approving a Payment

Vendor payments and farmer payments require Finance Director approval.

1. Click **Finance** then **Payments**.
2. Click the **Pending Approval** tab.
3. Click on a payment to open it.
4. Review the vendor, amount, bank account, and linked purchase order and goods receipt note.
5. Confirm the three-way match (Purchase Order, Goods Receipt Note, and Vendor Invoice) is shown as **Matched** at the top of the payment.
6. If everything is correct, click **Approve Payment**.
7. The system generates the bank transfer file or mobile money batch file for the payments team to execute.
8. If incorrect, click **Reject** and enter the reason.
