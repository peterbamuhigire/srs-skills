# Section 2: Samuel — Field Sales Agent

Samuel uses the Sales Agent App on his Android phone. The app works offline when there is no internet connection. All data syncs to the main system when he reconnects.

---

## 2.1 Using the Sales Agent App Offline

The Sales Agent App stores all your assigned products and prices on your phone. You do not need the internet to make a sale.

1. Open the **Sales Agent App** on your phone.
2. If the status bar at the top shows a green cloud icon, you are online. If it shows an orange cloud with a line through it, you are offline.
3. You can sell, record deliveries, and check your balances whether online or offline.
4. When you regain connectivity, the app syncs automatically. You can also tap **Sync Now** in the menu to trigger a sync immediately.

*Never uninstall the app when you have unsynced sales. You will lose those records.*

---

## 2.2 Processing a Sale on Mobile

1. Open the Sales Agent App and tap **New Sale**.
2. Tap inside the **Search Product** box. Type the product name or scan the barcode with your phone camera.
3. Tap the product to add it to the sale.
4. Adjust the **Quantity** if needed.
5. Add more products if the customer is buying multiple items.
6. Tap **Payment**.
7. Enter the **Amount Received** from the customer.
8. Tap **Complete Sale**.
9. If your Bluetooth thermal printer is connected and switched on, the receipt prints automatically. If not, tap **Send via WhatsApp** to send the customer a digital receipt.

*Your stock balance in the app reduces when you complete a sale. If a product is out of stock, the app will not let you sell it.*

---

## 2.3 Submitting a Cash Remittance

At the end of the day (or whenever you have collected enough cash), submit your remittance to the office.

1. In the Sales Agent App, tap **Submit Remittance**.
2. Enter the **Amount** you are remitting.
3. Select the **Payment Method**: cash (if delivering to the office) or mobile money.
4. If paying by mobile money, confirm the BIRDC remittance number shown on screen.
5. Tap **Submit**.
6. A remittance reference number appears. Note it down.
7. The remittance is automatically allocated to your oldest unpaid invoices (oldest first). You can see the allocation in the **Remittance History** screen.

*Your agent cash balance only reduces after a supervisor verifies your remittance. Submitting a remittance does not immediately reduce your balance — verification must happen first.*

---

## 2.4 Checking Your Commission Statement

1. In the Sales Agent App, tap **My Account**.
2. Tap **Commission Statement**.
3. Select the date range you want to view.
4. Your commission for each verified remittance period is shown.
5. Tap any row for the breakdown of which invoices contributed to that commission amount.

*Commission is earned only on verified sales — sales linked to a remittance that a supervisor has confirmed. Unverified remittances do not generate commission.*

---

## 2.5 Checking Your Outstanding Cash Balance

Your outstanding cash balance is the total amount you still owe BIRDC from your sales.

1. In the Sales Agent App, tap **My Account**.
2. Tap **Cash Balance**.
3. The screen shows:
   - Total sales collected (all invoices issued to your customers)
   - Total remittances verified
   - Outstanding balance (what you still owe)
4. Tap **View Breakdown** to see the list of individual invoices that make up the balance.

*If your balance looks different from your manual calculation, check the Remittance History screen. A remittance you submitted may not yet be verified by a supervisor.*
