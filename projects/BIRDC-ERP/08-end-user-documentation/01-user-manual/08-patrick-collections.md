# Section 8: Patrick — Collections Officer (Cooperative Procurement)

Patrick uses the Farmer Delivery App on his Android phone. He travels to rural collection points where there is little or no internet connection. Everything he records is stored on his phone and synced when he returns to an area with connectivity.

---

## 8.1 Registering a New Farmer (Offline)

If a farmer arrives at the collection point and is not yet in the system:

1. Open the Farmer Delivery App and tap **Farmers**.
2. Tap **Register New Farmer**.
3. Fill in the following fields:
   a. **Full Name**
   b. **National Identification Number (NIN)** — required
   c. **Phone Number** (for mobile money payment)
   d. **Cooperative** — select from the list already stored on your phone
   e. **GPS Coordinates** — tap **Get My Location** to record where you are standing (the farm or collection point)
   f. **Photo** — tap the camera icon and take the farmer's photo
4. Tap **Save Farmer**. The farmer's record is saved on your phone.
5. When you sync, the farmer's record uploads to the main system and gets a permanent farmer ID.

*You can record deliveries from a newly registered farmer before syncing. The delivery is linked to the local record and will match up when you sync.*

---

## 8.2 Recording a Batch Delivery

1. Open the Farmer Delivery App and tap **Record Delivery**.
2. Select the **Cooperative** and the **Batch** (your supervisor will have told you the batch number before you left).
3. Search for the farmer by name or NIN. If the farmer is not found, register them first (see Section 8.1).
4. If a Bluetooth weighing scale is paired to your phone, place the matooke on the scale. Tap **Get Weight** to read the weight automatically. Otherwise, type the weight manually.
5. Select the **Quality Grade**: A, B, or C.
6. The app calculates the net amount payable to the farmer based on the grade price stored on your phone.
7. Tap **Add to Batch**. The delivery is recorded.
8. If your Bluetooth printer is on, a receipt prints automatically. Hand it to the farmer.
9. If the printer is not available, tap **Send Receipt via SMS** to send the farmer their receipt by text message.

---

## 8.3 Printing a Farmer Receipt

The app prints a receipt automatically after every delivery (Section 8.2). If you need to reprint a receipt:

1. Tap **Delivery History**.
2. Find the delivery in the list (search by farmer name or date).
3. Tap the delivery record.
4. Tap **Reprint Receipt**.
5. Make sure the Bluetooth printer is switched on and paired.
6. Tap **Print**. The receipt prints.

---

## 8.4 Syncing When Connectivity Returns

The app syncs automatically the moment it detects an internet connection. You can also sync manually:

1. Open the Farmer Delivery App.
2. Tap the menu icon (three lines at the top left).
3. Tap **Sync Now**.
4. A progress bar shows the upload status.
5. When sync is complete, the status bar at the top shows a green cloud icon.
6. Any new farmer registrations are assigned permanent farmer IDs by the main system and update on your phone.

*Always sync before the end of each field trip. Do not go days without syncing — if your phone is lost or damaged, unsynced records cannot be recovered.*
