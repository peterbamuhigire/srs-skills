## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document defines the **Definition of Done (DoD)** for the BIRDC ERP project. The DoD is a binding quality contract: a task, feature, or fix is not complete until every applicable criterion in this document is satisfied. No item may be marked complete, demonstrated to the client, or counted toward milestone acceptance unless the DoD is fully met.

The DoD applies to all work performed by Peter Bamuhigire and all hired developers on this engagement. It is reviewed at every milestone kickoff and may be updated by written agreement between Peter Bamuhigire and the BIRDC Director.

The DoD is divided into two levels:

- **Task-level DoD** — applies to every individual feature, bug fix, or change, regardless of size.
- **Milestone-level DoD** — applies additionally when claiming that a full delivery milestone is complete and ready for formal client sign-off.
