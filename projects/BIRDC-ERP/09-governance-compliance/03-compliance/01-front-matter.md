## Purpose

This document records how the BIRDC ERP system addresses each applicable regulatory framework and standard. For each regulatory obligation, it states the specific system mechanism — which module, which FR, which business rule — that satisfies the obligation. Where a gap exists, the document flags it for resolution.

## Applicable Regulatory Framework

| Regulation / Standard | Governing Body | Application to BIRDC |
|---|---|---|
| Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019 | National Data Protection Office (NDPO) | Farmer PII: NIN, GPS, photo, mobile money; employee data |
| Uganda PPDA Act (Cap 305) | PPDA Uganda | All BIRDC procurement — documentation, approval thresholds, audit trail |
| Uganda Income Tax Act (Cap 340) | URA | PAYE, WHT (6%), 7-year record retention |
| Uganda NSSF Act | NSSF Uganda | Mandatory contributions: employer 10% / employee 5% |
| Uganda Companies Act (Cap 110) | URSB | Financial record keeping and 7-year audit trail retention |
| IFRS for SMEs | ICPAU | Commercial financial reporting for BIRDC |
| ISO 22000:2018 | ISO / MAAIF | Food safety management for export markets |
| Codex Alimentarius | FAO / WHO | International food standards referenced in export CoA |
| OAG Uganda Audit Requirements | Office of the Auditor General | Annual audit of PIBID parliamentary accounts |
