## Purpose

This risk assessment identifies, categorises, quantifies, and mitigates all material risks to the BIRDC ERP project. It follows a structured risk register methodology with probability-impact scoring. Each risk is assigned an owner responsible for executing the mitigation strategy and tracking residual risk.

## Risk Scoring Matrix

Probability and impact are each rated on a 3-point scale: Low (1), Medium (2), High (3), Critical (4 — reserved for impact only). Risk Level = Probability × Impact.

| Risk Level Score | Classification |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Low |
| 3–4 | Medium |
| 6–8 | High |
| 9–12 | Critical |

## Risk Categories

- **Technical (T)** — technical design, integration, or infrastructure risks
- **External (E)** — regulatory, vendor, or environmental dependencies outside project control
- **Operational (O)** — operational and organisational risks affecting delivery or adoption
- **Legal (L)** — legal, regulatory, or compliance risks
