The following 3 risks require immediate action before Phase 1 development begins:

1. **R-002 (DPPA Legal Review — Critical):** BIRDC Director to commission DPPA legal counsel. This is a legal prerequisite, not a technical one, and has the longest lead time.
2. **R-001 (EFRIS Sandbox — High):** Peter to initiate URA EFRIS developer portal registration in Week 1 of Phase 1. Delays compound across the project timeline.
3. **R-006 (Developer Availability — High):** Peter to begin developer recruitment immediately, in parallel with Phase 1 documentation finalisation.

## 3.4 Risk Review Schedule

This risk register shall be reviewed and updated at every phase gate. The BIRDC Director, Finance Director, and Peter shall confirm:

1. Whether any open risks have been resolved.
2. Whether any new risks have materialised.
3. Whether any risk scores have changed.

A risk register update is a mandatory artefact for each phase gate sign-off package.
