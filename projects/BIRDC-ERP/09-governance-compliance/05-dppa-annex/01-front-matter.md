## Purpose and Scope

This annex documents all system requirements and controls that the BIRDC ERP system must implement to comply with the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019 (No. 9 of 2019) and the Data Protection and Privacy Regulations 2021. It covers all modules in the BIRDC ERP that collect, store, process, or transmit personal data or special personal data belonging to cooperative farmers, employees, and field agents.

This annex is a mandatory governance artifact. No module that processes personal data shall enter production without all requirements in this annex being implemented and independently verified.

---

## Applicability

This annex applies to:

- All phases of the BIRDC ERP system (Phase 1 through Phase 7)
- All Android mobile applications (Farmer Delivery App, HR Self-Service App, Sales Agent App, Factory Floor App, Warehouse App, Executive Dashboard App)
- All data processor relationships: MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, Africa's Talking
- BIRDC IT infrastructure at Nyaruzinga, Bushenyi
