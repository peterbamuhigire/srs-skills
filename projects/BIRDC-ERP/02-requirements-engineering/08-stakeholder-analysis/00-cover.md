## Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive stakeholder analysis for the BIRDC ERP system. It establishes the identity, influence, interest, and engagement strategy for all 29 identified stakeholders, and provides the planning artefacts — Power/Interest Grid, RACI Matrix, Communication Plan, and Risk/Concern Register — required to manage stakeholder engagement throughout the 7-phase delivery programme.

The analysis is used by the consultant (Peter Bamuhigire) and BIRDC project leadership to ensure that every stakeholder receives the right information, at the right time, through the right channel, and that no stakeholder's requirements or concerns are overlooked during the specification, build, or deployment phases.
