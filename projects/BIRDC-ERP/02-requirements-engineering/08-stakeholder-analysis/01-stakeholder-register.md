# Section 1: Stakeholder Register

The register below identifies all 29 stakeholders to the BIRDC ERP programme. Influence Level and Interest Level are scored on a 1–5 scale where 1 = minimal and 5 = critical.

**Influence Level** measures the stakeholder's formal authority or practical ability to affect the project outcome (approve funding, block delivery, or redirect scope).

**Interest Level** measures how directly and materially the stakeholder's daily work or organisational mandate is affected by the system.
