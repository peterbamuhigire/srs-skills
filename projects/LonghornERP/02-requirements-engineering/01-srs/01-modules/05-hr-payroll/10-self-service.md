# Employee Self-Service Portal

## 10.1 Payslip Access

**FR-HR-063** — When an employee logs into the Employee Self-Service (ESS) portal, the system shall display their last 3 payslips with a download link for the PDF; the system shall require the employee's own authentication token and shall not permit viewing another employee's payslips.

**FR-HR-064** — The ESS portal shall display the employee's year-to-date (YTD) gross pay, total PAYE deducted, total NSSF contributions, and net pay for the current financial year.

## 10.2 Leave Requests via ESS

**FR-HR-065** — The ESS portal shall allow an employee to submit a leave request, view pending and approved leave requests, and check their current leave balance per leave type; the request shall enter the same approval workflow as requests submitted via the HR back-office.

**FR-HR-066** — The ESS portal shall display a 12-month leave calendar showing the employee's approved leave days, public holidays, and tentative (pending approval) requests.

## 10.3 Attendance View

**FR-HR-067** — The ESS portal shall display the employee's attendance record for the current and previous month, showing daily status (Present, Absent, Leave, Holiday), clock-in time, clock-out time, and total hours worked per day.

**FR-HR-068** — The ESS mobile interface shall support clock-in and clock-out via the mobile application, with GPS capture as defined in FR-HR-021; the self-service attendance feature shall operate in offline mode and sync when connectivity is restored.

## 10.4 Profile Update Requests

**FR-HR-069** — The ESS portal shall allow an employee to submit an update request for personal contact details (phone number, email, residential address, next-of-kin); the request shall not apply automatically but shall be routed to an HR administrator for review and approval.
