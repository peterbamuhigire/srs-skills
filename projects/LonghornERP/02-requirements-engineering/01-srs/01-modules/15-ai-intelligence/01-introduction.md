# Introduction to the AI Intelligence Module SRS

## 1.1 Purpose

This Software Requirements Specification (SRS) defines the functional and non-functional requirements for the AI Intelligence Module of Longhorn ERP, a multi-tenant Software as a Service (SaaS) enterprise resource planning platform developed by Chwezi Core Systems. It covers five AI feature groups — cash flow forecasting, GL anomaly detection, demand forecasting, debtor risk scoring, and narrative financial reports — in sufficient detail to permit design, implementation, and verification.

## 1.2 Scope

The AI Intelligence Module is an optional paid add-on available on Professional, Business, and Enterprise subscription plans. It is disabled by default. All AI features operate exclusively on the tenant's own transaction data; no tenant data is shared across tenants or transmitted to third-party services except where the tenant has explicitly configured a Claude API key for narrative report generation (FR-AI-005).

## 1.3 Definitions, Acronyms, and Abbreviations

The following terms are used throughout this document per IEEE Std 610.12-1990 definitions unless otherwise noted.

## 1.6 Business Goals

The following business goals govern requirement priority and traceability throughout this SRS.

- **BG-AI-001:** Prevent cash flow crises by providing Finance Directors with 90-day forward cash position visibility.
- **BG-AI-002:** Detect internal fraud and unusual posting patterns through automated GL anomaly classification.
- **BG-AI-003:** Eliminate stockouts and reduce over-stocking by generating data-driven reorder recommendations.
- **BG-AI-004:** Reduce bad debt exposure by gating credit sales on a real-time customer payment risk score.
- **BG-AI-005:** Make monthly financial accounts intelligible to non-accountant leadership through plain-English management commentary.

## 1.7 Assumptions and Dependencies

- The tenant has completed onboarding and has at least one of the Professional, Business, or Enterprise subscription plans active.
- FR-AI-001 depends on AR, AP, HR & Payroll, and Accounting modules having transaction history present.
- FR-AI-002 depends on the Accounting module's journal posting system and the Audit Log module.
- FR-AI-003 depends on the Inventory and Sales modules having at least 90 days of sales movement history.
- FR-AI-004 depends on the Sales module's credit sales order workflow and the AR aging data.
- FR-AI-005 depends on the Accounting module's period-close status and optionally on a tenant-configured Claude API key.
- The Billing & Subscriptions platform service enforces plan-level feature gating; this module trusts the gate.
