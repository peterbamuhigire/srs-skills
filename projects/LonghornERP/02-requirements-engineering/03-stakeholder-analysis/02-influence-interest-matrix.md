## Influence-Interest Matrix for Longhorn ERP

This matrix classifies the 10 stakeholder personas across 4 engagement quadrants derived from the Power-Interest Grid (Mendelow, 1981). *Influence* measures a stakeholder's authority to shape requirements, budget, or release decisions. *Interest* measures the degree to which the platform's output directly affects the stakeholder's day-to-day work.

**Quadrant definitions:**

- **Manage Closely** — High Influence / High Interest: primary engagement targets; all requirements must be traceable to their stated needs.
- **Keep Satisfied** — High Influence / Low Interest: engaged at key milestones to prevent blocking decisions; not involved in sprint-level detail.
- **Keep Informed** — Low Influence / High Interest: frequent communication ensures adoption; feedback is captured but does not unilaterally gate delivery.
- **Monitor** — Low Influence / Low Interest: passive stakeholders; notified of outcomes that affect them.

| ID | Stakeholder | Quadrant | Engagement Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| STK-01 | Finance Manager / Accountant | Manage Closely | Participate in all Finance module requirements workshops and review every draft of accounting and VAT specifications before sign-off. |
| STK-02 | Warehouse / Inventory Officer | Manage Closely | Participate in Inventory module workshops; validate GRN and stock-transfer workflows via user acceptance testing (UAT) prototypes. |
| STK-03 | Sales Representative / Field Agent | Keep Informed | Receive sprint demo invitations for Sales and CRM modules; submit feedback via structured survey after each demo. |
| STK-04 | HR / Payroll Officer | Manage Closely | Participate in HR and Payroll module workshops; review statutory compliance rules (NSSF, PAYE) before each Phase 1 milestone gate. |
| STK-05 | Operations / Procurement Manager | Manage Closely | Co-author the procurement approval matrix; validate three-way matching logic and PPDA audit-trail requirements before Phase 1 sign-off. |
| STK-06 | CEO / MD | Keep Satisfied | Receive an executive summary at each phase gate and a live dashboard demonstration at Phase 1 launch; not involved in sprint-level review. |
| STK-07 | System Administrator (Tenant) | Keep Informed | Receive administrator documentation and a walkthrough of role and module configuration screens before go-live; provide feedback on onboarding UX. |
| STK-08 | Super Admin (Chwezi Platform) | Manage Closely | Participates in all platform-level decisions (tenant provisioning, billing, impersonation); has final approval authority on infrastructure and security requirements. |
| STK-09 | External Auditor | Keep Informed | Receives read-only environment credentials and audit-log documentation before the compliance review window; no workshop participation required. |
| STK-10 | Cooperative / Outgrower Farmer | Monitor | Notified of farmer portal availability via SMS broadcast; feedback collected through cooperative liaison officer rather than direct engagement. |
