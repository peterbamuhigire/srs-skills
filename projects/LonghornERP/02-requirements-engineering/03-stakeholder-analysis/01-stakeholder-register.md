## Stakeholder Register for Longhorn ERP

This register identifies the 10 stakeholder personas whose needs the Longhorn Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) platform will address. Each entry maps the stakeholder to their organisational context, primary requirements, and a measurable success criterion that the platform shall satisfy.

| ID | Stakeholder | Role | Organisation | Primary Needs | Success Metric |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| STK-01 | Finance Manager / Accountant | Financial Operations Lead | Tenant organisation | Double-entry general ledger (GL), financial statements, VAT returns, bank reconciliation, budget management, Uganda Revenue Authority (URA) Electronic Fiscal Receipting and Invoicing Solution (EFRIS) integration | Monthly close completed in < 2 hours without external consultant |
| STK-02 | Warehouse / Inventory Officer | Stock Control Lead | Tenant organisation | Real-time stock levels, Goods Received Note (GRN) processing, reorder alerts, inter-branch stock transfers, batch and lot tracking, barcode scanning | Zero unexplained stock variances; GRN processed in < 5 minutes |
| STK-03 | Sales Representative / Field Agent | Revenue Generation | Tenant organisation | Customer records, invoice creation, payment recording, commission visibility, mobile access, Customer Relationship Management (CRM) pipeline | New invoice created from mobile device in < 3 minutes |
| STK-04 | HR / Payroll Officer | Human Resources Lead | Tenant organisation | Employee records, leave management, payroll run, National Social Security Fund (NSSF) and Pay As You Earn (PAYE) compliance, statutory report generation | Monthly payroll run completed in < 30 minutes for 100 employees |
| STK-05 | Operations / Procurement Manager | Supply Chain Lead | Tenant organisation | Purchase requisitions, Local Purchase Order (LPO) creation, three-way matching, supplier management, Public Procurement and Disposal of Public Assets Authority (PPDA) audit trail | Purchase order approved and dispatched within 24 hours of requisition |
| STK-06 | Chief Executive Officer (CEO) / Managing Director (MD) | Executive Decision-Maker | Tenant organisation | Dashboard key performance indicators (KPIs), financial summaries, Balanced Scorecard (BSC) view, project profit and loss (P&L) | Full business overview available on mobile dashboard at any time |
| STK-07 | System Administrator (Tenant) | Platform Configuration Owner | Tenant organisation | User creation, role assignment, module activation, branch setup, self-service configuration without vendor support | New user onboarded and productive in < 15 minutes |
| STK-08 | Super Admin (Chwezi Platform) | Platform Operations Owner | Chwezi Core Systems | Tenant provisioning, subscription management, billing, impersonation for support escalations | New tenant provisioned and live in < 10 minutes |
| STK-09 | External Auditor | Independent Assurance Provider | Third-party audit firm | Read-only access to audit logs, financial statements, GL detail, transaction drill-down | Full audit trail accessible without requiring vendor support |
| STK-10 | Cooperative / Outgrower Farmer | End Beneficiary | Agricultural cooperative or outgrower scheme | Commodity intake records, deduction statements, mobile money payment confirmation via end-user portal | Payment statement accessible from a basic Android device |
