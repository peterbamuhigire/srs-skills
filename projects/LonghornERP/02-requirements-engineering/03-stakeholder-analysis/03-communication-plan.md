## Communication Plan for Longhorn ERP

This plan defines the information each stakeholder group will receive, the frequency of delivery, and the channel through which it will be distributed. All prospective tenses reflect delivery commitments that the Longhorn ERP platform and project team will fulfil during and after Phase 1.

### Principles

- Communication artefacts will be tailored to the technical literacy of each audience. Executive documents will not contain schema detail; operational documents will not contain financial narrative.
- All scheduled reports will be generated by the system automatically where the data exists. Manual compilation will be limited to artefacts that require consultant narrative.
- Push notifications will be used for time-sensitive alerts (e.g., approval requests, payment confirmations). Scheduled reports will be delivered by email or in-app inbox.
