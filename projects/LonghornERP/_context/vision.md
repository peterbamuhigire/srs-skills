## One-Line Description

A multi-tenant, modular SaaS enterprise resource planning platform that is simpler to use, easier to configure, and more affordable than any comparable ERP — targeting mid-sized and growing organisations across East Africa and globally.

## Golden Rule

If a user would need a manual or a consultant to complete a task, the interface is redesigned until they do not. Zero mandatory training.

## Flagship Status

Longhorn ERP is the flagship product of Chwezi Core Systems. It is the primary commercial vehicle for the company and is designed to serve as many clients as possible across all sectors.

## Strategic Goals

1. Dominate the Uganda ERP market as the default choice for mid-sized organisations.
2. Expand across East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, South Sudan, Burundi) within 24 months of launch.
3. Enter Francophone Africa (DRC, Cameroon, Côte d'Ivoire, Senegal, Mali, Burkina Faso) within 36 months.
4. Achieve global configurability — any country addable via localisation profile with no code changes.
5. Beat Odoo and ERPNext on ease of use; beat SAP and Oracle on price.
6. Integrate with every major mobile money provider in East and Central Africa.
7. Provide native mobile apps (Android, iOS) feeding the same ERP backend.
8. Expose a public API so that external systems can integrate with Longhorn ERP.

## Target Markets

**Industry-agnostic by design.** Priority sectors:

- Manufacturing (food processing, agro-processing, beverages, dairy, flour milling)
- Trading and distribution (FMCG, hardware wholesalers, pharmaceutical distributors)
- Agricultural cooperatives (tea, coffee, sugarcane, cotton, matooke, outgrower schemes)
- Service businesses (consulting, law, accounting, NGOs)
- Retail and POS (supermarkets, pharmacies, hardware shops)
- Hospitality (hotels, lodges, restaurants, bars)
- NGOs and donor-funded organisations
- Government and parastatals (PPDA compliance)
- Professional services (engineering, construction, architects)
- Schools and universities (integrates with Academia Pro)

## Methodology

Hybrid — iterative development with formal phase-gate sign-off before each market launch.

## Module Philosophy

All modules are universal. Localisation (country-specific tax, statutory, currency, and regulatory behaviour) is a configuration layer, not a module variant. Phase 1 ships all modules.

## Integration Ecosystem
