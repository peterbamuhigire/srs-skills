*All prices in Ugandan Shillings (UGX). USD equivalents calculated at UGX 3,750 per USD. Annual billing: 10 months charged, 12 months access (2 months free).*

## Pricing Rationale

1. **UGX denomination removes exchange rate exposure.** Ugandan businesses budget in UGX. A USD-priced subscription becomes an unpredictable line item whenever the shilling moves. Longhorn ERP eliminates this by pricing entirely in UGX, enabling accurate 12-month cost forecasting regardless of currency fluctuations.

2. **Organisation-wide pricing is the primary differentiator.** Odoo Enterprise and Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central charge per active user. A 30-user organisation on Odoo pays approximately UGX 4.3M/month; the same organisation on Longhorn ERP Professional pays UGX 1,000,000/month — a 4× saving before accounting for implementation fees. As a client grows from 10 to 30 users, the Longhorn ERP bill does not change.

3. **Starter at UGX 250,000/month is attainable.** For a 3–5 person trading company or professional services firm, UGX 250,000/month is comparable to the monthly airtime budget for the office team. It represents less than 0.5% of UGX 60M annual revenue for a micro-enterprise. The Starter tier removes the financial barrier to entry for organisations that have never used ERP software before.

4. **Enterprise at UGX 2,500,000/month is a fraction of the competitive cost.** Odoo Enterprise with comparable user count costs approximately UGX 4.3M/month in software alone, before implementation fees of UGX 75M–300M. Longhorn ERP Enterprise at UGX 2,500,000/month includes dedicated onboarding and priority support with no implementation fee — making the total first-year cost lower than Odoo's first-month cost in many scenarios.

5. **Annual billing creates a 2-month incentive.** A client billed annually pays for 10 months and receives 12 months of access. This yields an effective monthly rate of 83.3% of the list price, a saving of UGX 500,000–5,000,000 per year depending on tier. Annual billing also reduces churn by increasing the switching cost at renewal time.

6. **Add-on stacking creates a natural Enterprise upgrade trigger.** A Professional plan subscriber (UGX 1,000,000/month) who activates all 10 add-ons pays UGX 1,000,000 + UGX 1,490,000 = UGX 2,490,000/month — UGX 10,000 below Enterprise and without the unlimited users, unlimited branches, dedicated onboarding, or priority support that Enterprise includes. This price gap makes the upgrade argument self-evident.

## Accepted Payment Methods

- Debit or credit card (Visa, Mastercard) via the billing portal
- Bank transfer to the Chwezi Core Systems business account
- MTN MoMo Business Pay
- M-Pesa Lipa na M-Pesa (Kenya)

Payment is due on the first of each billing period. Accounts overdue by more than 7 days enter a grace period with restricted access. Accounts overdue by more than 30 days are suspended. Data is retained for 90 days post-suspension before archival.
