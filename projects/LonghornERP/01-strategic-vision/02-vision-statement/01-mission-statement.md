# Mission Statement — Chwezi Core Systems

Chwezi Core Systems shall design and deliver modular, cloud-native software platforms purpose-built for mid-sized and growing organisations in East Africa and beyond. Every product in the Chwezi Core suite shall eliminate the cost barriers, implementation friction, and usability gaps that have historically locked African businesses out of enterprise-grade software — replacing those barriers with organisation-wide flat pricing, zero mandatory training, and native integrations with the mobile money, tax, and regulatory infrastructure that African businesses operate within every day. The company's primary commercial vehicle, Longhorn Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP), shall serve organisations across every productive sector — manufacturing, trade, agriculture, services, hospitality, education, and government — and shall expand market by market until any organisation on the continent can deploy a fully capable ERP within a single business day without engaging a consultant.

*The mission of Chwezi Core Systems is to make enterprise-grade software unconditionally accessible to African businesses — affordable without compromise, usable without training, and compliant without customisation.*
