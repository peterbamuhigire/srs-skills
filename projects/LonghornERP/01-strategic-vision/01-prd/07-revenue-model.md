# Revenue Model

## Subscription Tiers

All prices are in Ugandan Shillings (UGX). No per-seat fees apply at any tier. The price covers the entire organisation.

| Plan | UGX/month | USD equiv. | Users | Branches | Add-Ons Included |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 250,000 | ~$67 | Up to 5 | Up to 2 | Core 6 modules only |
| Small Business | 500,000 | ~$133 | Up to 15 | Up to 5 | Core + any 2 add-ons |
| Professional | 1,000,000 | ~$267 | Up to 30 | Up to 10 | Core + any 5 add-ons |
| Business | 1,750,000 | ~$467 | Up to 60 | Up to 20 | Core + all add-ons |
| Enterprise | 2,500,000 | ~$667 | Unlimited | Unlimited | All modules + priority support + dedicated onboarding |

*USD equivalents at ~UGX 3,750/$1.*

*Starter locks out all add-on modules. Upgrade to Small Business or above to activate add-ons.*

## Annual Billing Incentive

Tenants who pay annually receive 12 months of access for the price of 10 months — 2 months free. Annual billing is pre-paid. No refunds are issued for early cancellation under annual contracts. Annual billing reduces churn and provides working capital for platform development.

| Billing Cycle | Charge | Effective Monthly Rate |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly | 12 × monthly rate | 100% of monthly rate |
| Annual | 10 × monthly rate | 83.3% of monthly rate |

## Add-On Stacking Upgrade Trigger

A Professional plan tenant activating all 10 add-ons would pay:

- Professional base: UGX 1,000,000/month
- All add-ons (POS counted as 1 terminal): UGX 1,490,000/month
- Total: UGX 2,490,000/month

This is UGX 10,000 below the Enterprise plan price of UGX 2,500,000/month. The system shall detect when a tenant's combined subscription cost (base + add-ons) reaches ≥ 95% of the next tier price and display a contextual upgrade prompt offering the higher tier for the price difference, with the benefit of unlimited users, unlimited branches, dedicated onboarding, and priority support.

## Revenue Model Summary

Longhorn ERP is a pure SaaS subscription business. There are no implementation fees, no licence fees, no module purchase costs, and no support contracts sold separately. Revenue is generated entirely from monthly or annual subscription payments. Add-on a-la-carte revenue supplements base subscription revenue and is structured to create natural upgrade paths to higher tiers.
