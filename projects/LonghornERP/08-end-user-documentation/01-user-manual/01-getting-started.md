# Getting Started with Longhorn ERP

This section applies to all users regardless of role. Complete these steps before using any module.

---

## Logging In

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Longhorn ERP URL provided by your system administrator.
2. On the login page, enter your **Username** (usually your work email address).
3. Enter your **Password**.
4. Click **Log In**.

If you cannot log in, contact your system administrator to confirm your account is active.

### First-Time Password Change

On your first login, the system requires you to change your temporary password.

1. After logging in with your temporary password, the **Change Password** screen appears automatically.
2. Enter your temporary password in the **Current Password** field.
3. Enter your new password in the **New Password** field. Your password must be at least 8 characters and include at least one number.
4. Re-enter the new password in the **Confirm Password** field.
5. Click **Update Password**.
6. The system logs you in and takes you to your dashboard.

---

## Dashboard Overview

After logging in, the dashboard is the first screen you see. It contains four main areas.

**KPI Cards** — Located at the top of the main content area. These cards show key numbers for your role, such as outstanding invoices, pending approvals, or open purchase orders. Click any card to open the relevant module.

**Left Sidebar Navigation** — The menu on the left side of the screen. Click a module name to expand it and reveal sub-menu items. The current page is highlighted in the sidebar.

**Top Bar** — The horizontal bar at the top of the screen. It contains:

- The Longhorn ERP logo and current branch name on the left.
- The **branch selector** dropdown in the centre.
- A bell icon for **notifications** on the right.
- Your profile avatar and name on the far right.

**Notifications Panel** — Click the bell icon in the top bar to open a slide-in panel listing recent alerts such as approvals waiting for your action, system messages, and low stock warnings.

---

## Navigating Between Modules

1. Locate the left sidebar on any screen.
2. Click the module group name (for example, **Finance** or **Inventory**) to expand the group.
3. Click the specific sub-module you want to open (for example, **Journal Entries** or **Stock Counts**).
4. The main content area loads the selected module. The sidebar highlights your current location.

**Breadcrumbs** appear just below the top bar and show your current path, for example: `Dashboard > Finance > Journal Entries`. Click any breadcrumb link to navigate back to that level.

---

## Changing Your Branch

If your account has access to more than one branch, use the branch selector to switch context.

1. Locate the **branch selector** dropdown in the top bar. It displays the name of your currently active branch.
2. Click the dropdown to open it.
3. Type a branch name in the search box to filter the list, or scroll to find the branch.
4. Click the branch name to select it.
5. The page reloads with data filtered to the selected branch.

*All transactions you create after switching will be recorded against the newly selected branch.*

---

## Logging Out

1. Click your profile avatar or name in the top-right corner of the top bar.
2. A small dropdown menu appears.
3. Click **Log Out**.
4. The system ends your session and returns you to the login page.

*Always log out when leaving your workstation, especially on shared computers.*
