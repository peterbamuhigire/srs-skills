# Field Agent Guide

**Role:** Field Agent

**Accessible platforms:** Sales Agent Web Portal, Longhorn ERP Mobile App

---

## Web Portal

### Viewing Your Assigned Sales Targets

1. Log in to the Sales Agent Portal at the URL provided by your administrator.
2. On the dashboard, the **Targets** panel shows your current month's sales target alongside your achieved sales to date.
3. In the sidebar, click **Targets** to see a full breakdown by product category or customer group.
4. Use the **Period** dropdown at the top to switch between monthly and quarterly views.

---

### Viewing Sales Attributed to You

1. In the sidebar, click **My Sales**.
2. Set the **From Date** and **To Date** using the date pickers.
3. Click **Generate**. The table shows each sale attributed to your agent code, including the customer name, invoice date, invoice amount, and payment status.
4. Click any row to open the sale detail.
5. Click **Export to Excel** to download the list.

---

### Viewing Your Commission Statement for the Current Month

1. In the sidebar, click **Commission**, then click **Commission Statement**.
2. The current month is selected by default. Use the **Period** dropdown to view a previous month.
3. The statement shows:
   - A list of qualifying sales.
   - The commission rate applied to each sale.
   - The commission amount earned per sale.
   - The total commission earned for the period.
   - The payment status: Pending, Approved, or Paid.
4. Scroll to the bottom of the page to see the total payable amount.

---

### Downloading a Commission Statement PDF

1. Open your commission statement as described above.
2. Click **Download PDF** at the top right of the statement page.
3. The PDF generates and downloads to your device. It includes your agent details, the period, and the full statement breakdown.

---

## Mobile App

### Logging In with Your Agent Credentials

1. Open the **Longhorn ERP** app on your phone.
2. Enter your **Username** (your registered phone number or email).
3. Enter your **Password**.
4. Tap **Log In**.
5. On first login, the app downloads your assigned customer list, product list, and commission settings for offline use. Wait for the sync to complete before going offline.

---

### Recording a Customer Visit and Sale

1. On the app home screen, tap **New Visit**.
2. Search for the customer by name or select from your assigned customer list.
3. Confirm the customer location. If prompted, allow the app to record your GPS location.
4. In the **Visit Notes** field, enter a summary of the discussion.
5. To record a sale, tap **Add Sale** within the visit.
6. Select the items sold and enter quantities.
7. Confirm the unit prices.
8. Tap **Save Sale**. The sale is stored on the device if you are offline and will sync when connectivity is restored.
9. Tap **Complete Visit** to close the visit record.

---

### Viewing Your Commission Balance Offline

1. On the app home screen, tap **Commission**.
2. The commission screen shows your last synced balance, the number of qualifying sales recorded on the device since the last sync, and an estimated commission for those sales.
3. The last sync date and time is displayed at the top of the screen. Data shown is current as of that sync.

---

### Syncing When Connectivity Is Restored

The app syncs automatically when it detects an internet connection. You can also sync manually.

1. On the app home screen, tap the **Sync** icon (circular arrows) in the top-right corner.
2. The app uploads all locally saved visits and sales to the server.
3. It then downloads any updates from the server: new customer records, updated targets, and the latest commission calculations.
4. A confirmation message shows the number of records uploaded and downloaded.
5. If any records fail to sync, a warning appears with a list of the failed items. Contact your administrator if sync errors persist.
