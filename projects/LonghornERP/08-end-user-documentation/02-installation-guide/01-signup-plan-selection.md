# Step 1: Sign Up and Choose Your Plan

## Creating Your Account

1. Open a web browser and go to `app.longhornERP.com/signup`.
2. Click **Get Started** to begin the registration process.
3. Enter your work email address and create a password, then click **Continue**.

## Choosing a Subscription Plan

Longhorn ERP offers 5 plans. Review the table below and select the one that fits your business.

| Plan | Monthly Price (UGX) | Users Included | Core Modules |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | 250,000 | Up to 5 | Finance, Inventory, Sales |
| Small Business | 500,000 | Up to 15 | Finance, Inventory, Sales, HR |
| Professional | 1,000,000 | Up to 30 | All core + Procurement, Projects |
| Business | 1,750,000 | Up to 75 | All Professional + CRM, POS |
| Enterprise | 2,500,000 | Unlimited | All modules + dedicated support |

Click **Select Plan** under your chosen option to continue.

## Billing Cycle

Choose how you would like to be billed:

- **Monthly billing** — charged each calendar month.
- **Annual billing** — pay for 10 months and receive 12 months of access (2 months free). Billed as a single annual payment.

Select your preferred billing cycle and click **Continue**.

## Entering Company Details

Fill in the following fields:

1. **Company Name** — the legal name of your business.
2. **Country** — select the country where your business is registered.
3. **Primary Currency** — the currency you use for day-to-day transactions (e.g., UGX, KES, TZS, RWF).
4. **Industry** — select the sector that best describes your business (e.g., Retail, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Education).

Click **Continue** when all fields are complete.

## Payment

Select your preferred payment method:

- **Card** — Visa or Mastercard. Enter your card number, expiry date, and CVV.
- **Bank Transfer** — you will receive Chwezi Core Systems' bank details by email. Your workspace activates once payment is confirmed (1–2 business days).
- **Mobile Money** — select MTN MoMo or M-Pesa, enter your mobile number, and approve the payment prompt on your phone.

Click **Complete Payment** to finalise your subscription.

## What Happens Next

After successful payment, Longhorn ERP will:

1. Create your dedicated workspace.
2. Send a welcome email to your registered address containing your workspace URL (e.g., `yourcompany.longhornERP.com`) and your administrator login credentials.
3. Prompt you to change your password on first login.

Keep your welcome email — it contains your workspace URL and initial credentials.
