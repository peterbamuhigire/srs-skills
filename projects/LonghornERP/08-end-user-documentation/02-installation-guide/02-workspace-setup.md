# Step 2: Set Up Your Workspace

When you log in for the first time, Longhorn ERP launches a setup wizard. Complete each step in order. You can return to any step later from **Settings**, but finishing the wizard before processing transactions ensures your data is configured correctly.

---

## Step 1 of 7 — Company Profile

1. Upload your company logo by clicking **Upload Logo**. Accepted formats: PNG or JPG, maximum 2 MB.
2. Confirm or edit your **Company Name** as it should appear on invoices and documents.
3. Enter your **Physical Address** and **Postal Address**.
4. Enter your **Tax Registration Number** — TIN for Uganda, PIN for Kenya, TIN/TRP for other countries. This number prints on all tax invoices.
5. Click **Save and Continue**.

---

## Step 2 of 7 — Localisation

1. Select your **Country** from the dropdown. This sets the applicable tax regime and statutory defaults.
2. Select your **Primary Currency** (UGX, KES, RWF, TZS, or other supported currencies).
3. Confirm the **Language** — currently English. Additional languages are on the product roadmap.
4. Click **Save and Continue**.

---

## Step 3 of 7 — Chart of Accounts

Your Chart of Accounts (COA) is the foundation of your financial records. Choose the option that best fits your jurisdiction:

| Template | Recommended For |
|---|---|
| Uganda COA (IFRS) | Businesses registered in Uganda |
| Kenya COA (IFRS) | Businesses registered in Kenya |
| OHADA COA | Francophone African countries |
| Blank | Advanced users who will build a custom COA |

1. Select your preferred template.
2. Click **Load Template**. Longhorn ERP populates the account list automatically.
3. Click **Save and Continue**. You can add, rename, or deactivate accounts later from **Accounting > Chart of Accounts**.

---

## Step 4 of 7 — Opening Balances

Opening balances capture the financial position of your business at the date you begin using Longhorn ERP.

1. Enter your **Cutover Date** — this is the date your books move from your previous system to Longhorn ERP.
2. For each Balance Sheet account with a balance as at that date, enter the amount in the **Opening Balance** column.
3. The wizard shows a running **Debit/Credit Difference** at the bottom of the page. This must equal zero before you can proceed.
4. Click **Save and Continue** once the difference is zero.

If you are migrating mid-year, also enter year-to-date figures for Profit & Loss accounts so that your reports reflect the full financial year correctly.

---

## Step 5 of 7 — Branches and Warehouses

1. Click **Add Branch** and enter the branch name and address.
2. For each branch, click **Add Warehouse** and enter:
   - **Warehouse Name** (e.g., Main Store, Bonded Store, Transit).
   - **Warehouse Type** — select Standard, Consignment, or Transit.
3. Add at least 1 branch and 1 warehouse before proceeding.
4. Click **Save and Continue**.

You can add more branches and warehouses at any time from **Settings > Organisation > Branches**.

---

## Step 6 of 7 — Users and Roles

1. Click **Add User** and enter the user's full name, email address, and phone number.
2. Select a **Role** for the user. Built-in roles include:

   - **System Administrator** — full access to all settings and modules.
   - **Finance Manager** — full access to accounting and reports; no access to HR payroll.
   - **Accountant** — journal entries, invoices, and payments; read-only reports.
   - **Inventory Officer** — goods receipts, stock transfers, and adjustments.
   - **Sales Representative** — quotations and sales orders; no pricing overrides.
   - **HR Manager** — employee records, payroll, and leave management.
   - **Procurement Officer** — purchase requests and purchase orders.

3. Click **Send Invite**. The user receives an email with a link to set their password.
4. Repeat for each user on your team.
5. Click **Save and Continue** when all users are added.

You can create custom roles with granular permissions from **Settings > Users & Roles > Role Permissions**.

---

## Step 7 of 7 — Module Activation

Longhorn ERP activates core modules automatically based on your plan. Add-on modules that are included in your plan but not active by default are listed here.

1. Review the list of available modules.
2. Toggle **On** next to each module you want to activate.
3. Click **Activate Selected Modules**.
4. Click **Finish Setup**.

Your workspace is now ready. You will land on the main dashboard.
