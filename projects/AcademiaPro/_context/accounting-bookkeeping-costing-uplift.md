# AcademiaPro Accounting and School Finance Uplift

## Purpose

This note applies accounting, bookkeeping, school-fee billing, grants/funds, payroll, and management reporting guidance to AcademiaPro requirements and design work.

## Product Implications

- Link fee billing, discounts, bursaries, scholarships, receipts, arrears, refunds, deposits, uniforms/books, transport, meals, hostel, exams, and penalties to controlled subledger and GL postings.
- Maintain student/guardian AR subledgers that reconcile to fee-control accounts and aging reports.
- Support fund/project tracking for grants, PTA funds, restricted donations, capital projects, and school programmes where applicable.
- Use cost centres for campus, school section, department, class stream, transport, boarding, meals, library, sports, and administration where management reporting requires it.
- Link procurement, payroll, supplier payments, fixed assets, and inventory to accounting controls and period close.
- Provide school-friendly dashboards: collections, arrears, scholarship cost, outstanding balances, fee category performance, cash position, budget vs actual, and department/campus cost.

## Requirement Prompt

Every AcademiaPro financial workflow should define payer, fee item, discount/waiver approval, receipt allocation, arrears treatment, refund/reversal path, GL posting, fund/cost-centre dimension, and reconciliation report.
