# Medic8 Accounting and Billing Uplift

## Purpose

This note applies accounting, bookkeeping, healthcare billing, pharmacy inventory accounting, and management reporting guidance to Medic8 requirements and design work.

## Product Implications

- Link registration fees, consultations, lab tests, pharmacy issues, inpatient charges, insurance claims, discounts, waivers, refunds, receipts, and bad-debt write-offs to controlled billing and accounting events.
- Maintain AR subledgers for patients, insurers, corporate clients, and schemes where credit or claims exist.
- Reconcile pharmacy inventory and COGS to stock movements, prescriptions, returns, expiries, wastage, and stock-count variance.
- Use cost centres for OPD, inpatient, lab, pharmacy, maternity, theatre, administration, facility, donor project, and branch where applicable.
- Support daily cashup, mobile money/bank reconciliation, claims aging, patient debtors, insurer aging, pharmacy stock value, department revenue, and management accounts.
- Ensure clinical audit trail and financial audit trail remain linked but not confused: clinical records prove care; finance records prove charge, receipt, claim, refund, or write-off.

## Requirement Prompt

Every chargeable clinical or pharmacy event should define its billing trigger, payer, revenue account, tax/exemption treatment where applicable, inventory/COGS effect, receipt or claim workflow, reversal path, and reconciliation report.
