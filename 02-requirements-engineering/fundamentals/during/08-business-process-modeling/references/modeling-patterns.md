# Modeling Patterns

Use this reference when structuring business process models.

## Minimum Elements

- Trigger
- Actors or swimlanes
- Ordered activities
- Decisions
- Exceptions
- Outputs or outcomes

## When To Prefer Each View

- Activity flow: role handoffs and branching matter
- Data flow: transformation and information movement matter
- Narrative step table: the audience is non-technical or notation-sensitive

## Validation Prompts

- Where does work wait?
- Who can override the normal flow?
- What happens when input is missing or invalid?
- Which step creates an auditable decision?
