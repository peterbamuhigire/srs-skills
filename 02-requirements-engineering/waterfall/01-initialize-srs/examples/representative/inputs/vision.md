# Vision

Hospital Admission System delivers measurable outcomes for healthcare operators.

- **BG-001** Reduce average patient admission time from 45 to 15 minutes by end of year 1.
- **BG-002** Maintain HIPAA Security Rule compliance across every deployed environment.

## Context

The hospital admits 400 patients per day across three wards. The current
paper-based intake flow produces a 2.3% duplicate-record rate and has
been flagged by the internal audit team.
